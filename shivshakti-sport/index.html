About Us

Shivshakti Sport Maskarwadi

Shivshakti Sport Maskarwadi is a powerful cricket initiative by the youth of Maskarwadi village. Inspired by the strength of Shivshakti, we showcase our energy, discipline and team spirit on the cricket field.

Our team not only plays cricket but also works to develop sports culture, health and a positive competitive spirit among the youth of the village. Coming together on the field, we recognize each other's strengths and weaknesses, support one another, and strive for victory.

The young generation of Maskarwadi is now making its mark on the cricket field. Shivshakti Sport is not just a team — it is a family, where every player matters.
